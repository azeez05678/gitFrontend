How to make Strawberry Ice Cream

  1. In a blender jar, add 1 cup of cold whole milk, Â¾ cup cold whipping cream (25% to 35% fat), 8 tablespoons sugar or as needed and 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries. Make sure to rinse the strawberries first in water a few times. Pat dry them thoroughly and then chop them.
  2. Blend everything to a smooth and fine mixture.
  3. Pour the ice cream mixture in a container. Cover tightly and keep in the freezer for the ice cream to set for 4 to 5 hours or as needed.
